VETERAN RESOURCES

DOCUMENTS AND FORMS

  • www.archives.gov/veterans - Retrieve military service records.
  • www.nrc-pad.org - Advanced Psychiatric Directive Forms.

WIDELY UNKNOWN VA BENEFITS

* Call your local VA and ask for contact information/websites for your state/local

  • HUD VASH - Housing assistance
  • Clothing Closet - Free clothing for low income Veterans. See social worker for voucher.
  • RRTP - Rehabilitation for addiction and mental issues (in-patient)

GENERAL WEBSITES

  • www.dav.org - Well established VSO to help with disability claims.
  • www.mightyoaksprograms.org - Various recovery camps and groups for veterans.
  • www.operationfirstresponse.org - Financial and family assistance for active duty and disabled veterans.
  • www.redcross.org/get-help/military-families/services-for-veterans.html - Claim assistance, caregiver network, links to several benefits.
  • www.operationhomefront.org - Connects donors with individual Veterans. List a request and get donations. Essentially a GoFundMe for Veterans.
  • www.legion.org - Various programs for assistance. Too many to list here.
  • www.usacares.org - Homeless, financial assistance.
  • www.vfw.org - Claims, grants, and other services (massive organization).
  • www.vetcenter.va.gov - Centers around the US that specialize in counseling of all types for veterans.
  • www.wwfs.org - Caregiver help, family retreats, welding training, mobility grants
  • www.fisherhouse.org - Housing, airline tickets, hotels & lodging, grants for nonprofits, scholarships for dependent spouses and children.
  • www.soldiersangels.org - Food assistance, hospital bed-side comfort, care packages for deployed, caregiver assistance.
  • https://teamneverquit.com/ - Support group for survivors and families of the fallen.
  • www.foldsofhonor.org - Scholarships.
  • www.vet2vetusa.org - Peer support for psychiatric conditions.
  • www.ffohf.org - Veteran’s benefits, job placement, housing, food assistance, transportation, and retreats for healing.
  • www.thearmsforces.org - Assistance to mentally wounded warriors. Psychological help.
  • www.legalhelpforveterans.com - Legal assistance with Veteran benefits.

SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS (combat, injured, rating %, etc.)

  • www.operationsecondchance.org - 70% or higher rating with < $4000 a month in income with honorable discharge. Retreats, scholarships, and financial assistance with necessities like housing and utilities.
  • www.veteransairlift.org - Free private air transportation for combat injured veterans for medical or other compassionate purposes.
  • www.militarywarriors.org - Housing, transportation, business, and training for wounded only.
  • www.saluteheroes.org - Employment resources, financial aid, retreats for OIF wounded veterans with a 30% or higher disability rating.
  • www.freedomalliance.org - Scholarships and other assistance for injured veterans and families.
  • www.woundedwarriorproject.org - Training and planning for health, finances, careers. For physically OR mentally wounded Veterans.
  • www.moonlightfund.org - Assistance to burn victims.
  • https://www.va.gov/education/about-gi-bill-benefits/post-9-11/yellow-ribbon-program - Additional VA assistance for those that qualify for the Post 9/11 GI Bill.
  • www.operationfamilyfund.org - Assistance for veterans of Operation Enduring Iraqi Freedom.
  • www.helpingahero.org - Various types of assistance for severely wounded veterans.
  • www.vacationsforveterans.org - Vacations for purple heart recipients.
  • www.catchaliftfund.org - Gym memberships, fitness assistance, and therapy for combat injured veterans.
